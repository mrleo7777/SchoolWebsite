Love Your School Auction

The "Love Your School" online auction is The Summit's annual fundraising event, directly benefiting every student and faculty member.

Proceeds raised immediately enrich the experience that both students and faculty have at The Summit. These funds extend The Summit's margin of excellence in this community and advance our mission of developing leaders of character.

The Summit "Love Your School" auction runs for two weeks in the spring. All bidding is done online.

With support from friends, donors, patrons, and community sponsors, we have raised significant funds throughout the years in support of the school. As a result of the success of previous auctions, The Summit has been able to purchase additional ActivBoards, classroom materials, supplies for the Montessori division, opportunities for Professional Development for numerous faculty members and many other resources.

Typically, the auction includes amazing trips, one of kind experiences, tasty treats, memorable gifts, unique offerings prepared by our students and faculty and much more.

If you have any questions about the auction please contact Lisa Nicholson, Auction Coordinator. Phone: (513) 871-4700 ext. 369. Email: nicholson_l@summitcds.org.
