The Summit has been a school of choice since 1890 for families in Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky who value academic excellence and character development. Private, Catholic and independent, The Summit is a leader in early childhood education. Students thrive in a Montessori preschool and are enriched by a college preparatory program through high school.
