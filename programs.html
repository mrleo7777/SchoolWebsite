Summit Summer

The Summit's summer school and day camp experience is called "Summit Summer." The program provides all day programming for children age 3 through grade eight. Classes and camps are also available for high school students, both for school credit and just an opportunity to explore a subject.

Piano and Violin

Individual instruction in violin and piano is offered during the school day to students in Kindergarten through Grade 12.
