Endowment: A Gift for Generations

The Summit Country Day School has earned a reputation as one of the nation's outstanding independent Catholic schools. You can help us continue this tradition of excellence and ensure the school's financial future by establishing an endowed fund. The interest generated by The Summit Endowment in honor of Edward C. Tyrrell supports four key areas of the school:

  • Scholarships and financial aid
  • Professional development, recruitment and retention of faculty and staff
  • Innovative academic programs, such as the Educating for Character program
  • Campus safety and beautification programs

Establish an Endowed Fund

You can make a difference in the future of The Summit by endowing a Named Fund. You can add to your Named Fund over time, and watch it grow to become part of the future success of The Summit.
